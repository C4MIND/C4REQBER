### 3.2 AGI Prohibition Clause
TURBO-CDI and its C4 Framework **may NOT** be used as a core component in the development of:

- Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) systems
- Self-improving AI systems without bounded, human-supervised improvement loops

**Rationale:** The C4 Framework represents a novel cognitive architecture. Its use in AGI development carries existential risk. This prohibition protects both humanity and the integrity of the framework.

> *"If the use case would cause harm to innocent people, erode democratic institutions, or contribute to existential risk, it is prohibited."*
